Fox HD
Social Media Dashboard
front-end development Fox HD is a multi-screen social media dashboard for Fox Network's headquarters in New York. Wendigo
CSS Experiment
  Just to see what would happen, I pushed the browser's ability to calculate nested positioning as far as I could. The timing of the animation is based on a factorization of a very large number, which gives the movement a spooky, organic quality.
